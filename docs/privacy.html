VolumeHub Chrome Extension

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 15, 2026

Short version: VolumeHub stores your audio preferences locally on your device only. No personal data is collected, transmitted, or shared — ever.

What VolumeHub Does

VolumeHub is a Chrome extension that lets you boost and control audio volume for any browser tab, apply a 3-band equalizer, and save per-site volume preferences.

Data We Collect

VolumeHub does not collect any personal data. The extension does not:

  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or crash-reporting services
  • Track browsing history or visited URLs beyond what is needed in the moment to read the current tab's domain
  • Store or log any audio content
  • Collect names, email addresses, or any identifying information

Data Stored Locally

VolumeHub saves the following settings to your browser's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local), which never leaves your device:

  • Per-domain volume levels — the volume you set for a given website
  • EQ settings — bass, mid, and treble adjustments
  • App preferences — dark mode, auto-apply toggle, default volume

This data is stored entirely on your device and is only accessible by the VolumeHub extension itself. It is never uploaded or synced to any external service.

Permissions Explained

VolumeHub requests only the permissions it needs to function:

  • tabs — to read the current tab's URL so the correct saved volume can be applied
  • tabCapture — to capture a tab's audio stream for volume boosting and EQ processing
  • storage — to save your preferences locally
  • offscreen — to run Web Audio API processing in an offscreen document, as required by Chrome's Manifest V3 architecture
  • alarms — to keep the background service worker alive while audio processing is active
  • Host permissions (<all_urls>) — required for tab audio capture to work on any website you visit

Third Parties

VolumeHub does not use any third-party libraries, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external services. No data of any kind is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

VolumeHub does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
